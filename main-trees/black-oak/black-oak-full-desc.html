Basics about Black Oak trees

  • Growing

    Planting Black Oak trees in rich soils and needs lots of sunlight

  • Things to Avoid when Growing

    Planting them near construction means a change in the grade around the root system of the tree. The seedlings will now survive in shade.

  • Height

    Black oak trees usually grows to about 80 feet tall (25 m.) and may grow to 148 feet (45 m.) on rick soils.
