Honey Locust

Basics about Honey Locust trees

Honey locusts are found in North and South America, tropical Africa, and central and eastern Asia.

  • Growing:

    Choose a sunny location, somewhere you want to add shade, and where you have rich and moist soil.

  • Things to Avoid when Growing:

    Good honey locust care includes management, prevention, and treatment for webworm, cankers, borers, powdery mildew and other pests or infections.

  • Height:

    The Honey Locust tree can grow up to 100 feet (30m.) and beyond, but in landscaping it usually tops out at 30 to 70 feet (9-21 m.)
